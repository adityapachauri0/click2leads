3D Growth Methodology Design Concepts

Concept 1: 3D Cubes with Perspective

This design features 3D cubes with proper perspective, shadows, and gradient effects. Each step is represented as a cube with top, side, and shadow faces. The cubes are connected with flowing arrows, and there's a subtle grid floor for depth perception.

Additional Design Elements

All concepts can include:

  • Floating particles for added depth
  • Glowing effects on hover
  • Smooth transitions between steps
  • Interactive tooltips with detailed information
  • WebGL shader backgrounds for advanced effects
  • Parallax scrolling for depth perception
